Ancient Greek Pottery Tileset

This was a piece I created when I took Graphic Design in Cartography at UW-Madison during Fall 2021. The inspiration for this project was derived from ancient Greek pottery and myths. Ever since middle school, Iâ€™ve been fascinated by the world of Greek mythology, so I decided to finally incorporate this interest into a cartographic project. I chose red-figure Greek pottery as my artistic inspiration because of the intricate linework and unique colors. For points of interest, I selected well-known locations in Greek mythology such as Mount Olympus and Athens. For each of these locations, I created custom icons with Adobe Illustrator.

Created using Adobe Illustrator, Mapbox Studio
