About Me

Hello! My name is Sydney, and I am a student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison working towards a bachelor's degree in Cartography and Geographic Information Systems and Physical Geography. I'm interested in biogeography and oceanography and how these two areas intersect. In the future, I hope to do research related to deep-sea mapping and marine ecosystems.
